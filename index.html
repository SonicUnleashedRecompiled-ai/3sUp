What is Nondisjunction?

Nondisjunction occurs when chromosomes fail to separate properly during cell division. Instead of each daughter cell receiving one chromosome from a pair, one cell may receive an extra copy while the other receives none. This imbalance can lead to genetic disorders or abnormal cell behavior.
